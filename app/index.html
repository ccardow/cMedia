What I Do

UI/UX Design

I've had a successful lifetime career taking complex concepts and simplifying them to simple metaphors. I use this ability in UI/UX design and have produced highly-intuitive web applications. I work primarily in Abobe XD to produce beautiful and effective prototypes which follow standard HTML standards, as well as utilize existing CSS frameworks, if required for rapid implementation.

Website Design

Whether in WordPress, using JavaScript front-end libraries, frameworks, or building from scratch with basic HTML5, flex and CSS, I have experience and the creative flair to produce beautiful websites.

HTML/CSS JavaScript

The knowledge of HTML5, CSS and JavaScript are the essential ingredients to being a good UI/UX practitioner. Understanding grids, box-sizing, global resets as well as the myriad of best practices is what sets great UI/UX designers apart from the rest of the pack.

Front-End Development

Front-end development is an art form. There are developers who implement from mockups "good enough," but a designer doing front-end work sees color, pixel-perfect design, balance, white-space and will ultimately produce work that will better represent the original design. As a designer, I appreciate how small imperfections of a website's implementation can diminish the overall effectiveness in reaching audiences.
